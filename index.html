About Us

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Nanyuki

For over four decades, Nanyuki Cottage Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare excellence in the region. Founded on principles of compassion and quality care, we have grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility serving the Nanyuki community and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and exceptional healthcare services in a peaceful, nature-rich environment.
