Our Story

40+ Years of Healthcare Excellence

Founded over four decades ago, Nanyuki Cottage Hospital has been a cornerstone of healthcare in Laikipia County. What began as a small medical facility has grown into a comprehensive healthcare institution serving thousands of patients annually.

Our journey is marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to patient care, and a deep understanding of our community's healthcare needs. We have consistently invested in modern medical equipment, staff training, and facility improvements to ensure we deliver world-class healthcare services.
